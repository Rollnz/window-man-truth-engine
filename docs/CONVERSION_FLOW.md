## Modal Conversion Strategy

### Modal Trigger Points

**High-Intent Triggers:**
1. Tool completion (especially high-risk results)
2. Comparison tool (shows big price differences)
3. Quote scanner (red flags detected)
4. Calculator (high delay costs shown)

**Timing:**
- Immediately after results screen
- User has invested 3-5 minutes (sunk cost)
- Results create urgency/concern
- Context is fresh

### Modal Types & Conversion Rates

**LeadCaptureModal**
- Appears after: 9 different tools
- Average open rate: 80%
- Conversion rate: 15-20%
- Fields: Email, Name, Phone (optional)
- Value prop: "Save your results + get personalized recommendations"

**ConsultationBookingModal**
- Appears after: Expert page, Intel page
- Average open rate: 60%
- Conversion rate: 25-30%
- Fields: Name, Email, Phone, Best time
- Value prop: "Book free 15-min expert consultation"

**IntelLeadModal**
- Appears after: Intel resource click
- Average open rate: 70%
- Conversion rate: 35-40%
- Fields: Email, First name
- Value prop: "Unlock this guide + get hurricane alerts"

### Modal Abandonment Analysis

**Common Abandonment Points:**
- 30% close immediately (not interested)
- 25% close after reading (considering)
- 20% fill partial form then close (friction)
- 25% complete form (conversion)

**Optimization Strategies:**
1. Reduce form fields (email only initially)
2. Add social proof in modal
3. Show value prop clearly
4. Offer "Skip for now" option
5. Retarget abandoners with ads

## Conversion Optimization Strategies

### A/B Testing Priorities

**Homepage:**
- Hero headline variations
- CTA button text ("Get Started" vs "Check Your Risk")
- Social proof placement
- Tool grid vs list layout

**Tool Pages:**
- Results screen layout
- Modal trigger timing
- Modal copy variations
- CTA button colors

**Landing Pages:**
- Headline formulas
- PDF mockup vs list of benefits
- Testimonial placement
- Form length (email only vs full info)

### Retargeting Campaigns

**Audience 1: Tool Users (No Lead)**
- Visited tool, didn't complete
- Retarget with: "Finish your assessment"
- Offer: Free PDF guide

**Audience 2: Tool Completers (No Lead)**
- Completed tool, didn't give email
- Retarget with: "Save your results"
- Offer: Vault trial + saved results

**Audience 3: Leads (No Vault)**
- Gave email, didn't start trial
- Retarget with: "Limited time offer"
- Offer: Extended trial (21 days)

**Audience 4: Vault Trials (Didn't Convert)**
- Started trial, didn't subscribe
- Retarget with: "Your documents are about to be deleted"
- Offer: 50% off first month

**Audience 5: Vault Subscribers (No Installation)**
- Paying subscribers, haven't booked
- Retarget with: "Expert review available"
- Offer: Free quote analysis
